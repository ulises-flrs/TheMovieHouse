Jumanji: The Next Level

Director: Jake Kasdan
Gender: Comedy, Action, Adventure
Launching: 1994
IMDb: 6.7/10

Sinopsis

Adventures continue in the fantastic world of the Jumanji game, where nothing is what it seems and where players cannot leave the game until the game is over. On this occasion, the same gang that we know returns but the game has changed: their characters have been exchanged among themselves, they are the same heroes but with different appearances. With the goal of rescuing one of their own, players will live exciting and fun adventures in unknown and unexplored places, from arid deserts to snowy mountains, to escape the most dangerous game in the world.
