Director: David F. Sandberg,
Walter Hamada,Geoff Johns
Gender: Action, Comedy,
Fantasy and Superheroes
Launching:2019
IMDb: 7.1/10

Sinopsis

Billy Batson, a troubled 14-year-old orphan boy living in Philadelphia, is ready to move into a new home, the seventh in a row, with the Vasquez family. One day, Billy boards a subway car and is transported to a different realm, where an ancient wizard gives him the power to transform into a divine adult superhero by uttering the word "Shazam!" Billy and his new foster brother Freddy Freeman must learn what Billy's new powers are and how to use them to prevent villainous Dr. Thaddeus Sivana from committing nefarious acts.
