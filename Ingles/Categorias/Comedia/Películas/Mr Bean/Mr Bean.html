Mr. Bean

Director: Steve Bendelack
Gender: Comedy
Launching: 2007
IMDb: 6.4/10

Sinopsis

Mr. Bean turns out to be the winner of a raffle in which the prize is a trip. His destination is the south of France, where he intends to spend a quiet and pleasant holiday in the sun. But that's a difficult thing for Mr Bean. Soon his journey is filled with his blunders and chaos, which he spreads wherever he goes without realizing it. He even goes as far as Cannes, where they inexplicably show a video of his trip at the famous festival. And, as is customary in him, he ends up turning France upside down.
