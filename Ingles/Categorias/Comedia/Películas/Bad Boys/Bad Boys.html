Bad Boys

Director: Adil El Arbi
Bilall Fallah
Gender: Comedy, Action
Launching: 2020
IMDb: 6.6/10

Sinopsis

Miami is once again the setting for this new installment of Two Rebel Policemen. There, detectives Mike Lowrey (Will Smith) and Marcus Burnett (Martin Lawrence) are at it again, and their new adventure will once again be full of action, gangs, car chases and explosions. With unique methods and a peculiar sense of coping with the problems and danger that abounds in the streets, including the constant arguments between the two, this pair of policemen will face new challenges and will always be prepared to break the rules when justice requires it.
