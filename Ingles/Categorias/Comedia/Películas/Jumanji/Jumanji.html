Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle.

Director: Jake Kasdan
Gender: Comedy/Action
Launching: 2017
IMDb: 6.9/10

Sinopsis

In the new adventure Jumanji: Into the Jungle, four teenagers who were punished at school are sucked into the world of Jumanji. When they discover an old console with a video game they had never heard of, they are transported to the jungle of the game in the bodies of their avatars played by Dwayne Johnson, Jack Black, Kevin Hart and Karen Gillan. What they discover is that you don't play Jumanji, but Jumanji plays you. They will have to embark on the most dangerous adventure of their lives or get stuck forever in the game.
