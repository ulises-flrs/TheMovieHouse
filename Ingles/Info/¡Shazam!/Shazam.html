Synopsis

Billy Batson, a 14-year-old, troubled orphan boy living in Philadelphia, is ready to move to a new home, the seventh consecutive, with the Vasquez family. One day, Billy takes a tube wagon and is transported to a different kingdom, where an ancient magician gives him the power to transform himself into a divine adult superhero by saying the word "Shazam!" Billy and his new adoptive brother Freddy Freeman must learn about Billy's new powers.
