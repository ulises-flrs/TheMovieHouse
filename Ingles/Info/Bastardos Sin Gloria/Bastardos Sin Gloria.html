Synopsis

It is the first year of the German occupation of France. The Allied officer, Lieutenant Aldo Raine, assembles a team of Jewish soldiers to commit violent acts against the Nazis, including taking scalps. He and his men join forces with Bridget von Hammersmark, a German actress and undercover agent, to overthrow the leaders of the Third Reich. Their destinies converge with the theater owner Shosanna Dreyfus, who seeks to avenge the execution of her family.
